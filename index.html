Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes Neura Bot different from other smart assistants?

A: Neura Bot combines advanced voice assistance with robotic mobility (arms and legs), fire resistance, and comprehensive safety systems, allowing it to perform physical tasks and offer unparalleled home security, unlike typical stationary smart speakers.

Q: How does Neura Bot clean the house?

A: Neura Bot uses integrated cleaning tools and intelligent navigation to dust, vacuum, and tidy various surfaces. It learns your home's layout and your cleaning preferences over time for efficient operation.

Q: Is my privacy protected with Neura Bot?

A: Absolutely. Neura Bot includes dedicated physical mute and volume buttons, giving you direct control over its audio inputs and outputs to ensure your privacy is always maintained.

Q: What kind of chores can Neura Bot do?

A: Neura Bot can assist with a variety of household chores, including light tidying, organizing items, assisting with laundry, and even helping with dish organization. Its capabilities expand as its AI learns more about your needs.

Q: How long does the battery last?

A: Neura Bot is designed for extended use, offering up to 100 hours of continuous operation on a single charge. It also intelligently manages its power to return to its charging station when needed.
